John Dies at the End


John Dies at the End is not a book for those with weak stomachs or a common sense of humor, but if you enjoy low-brow comedy and horror this might be good for you! From the editor of Cracked, David Wong has brought us a unique book documenting the fictional adventures of himself and his best friends, John and Amy. In the first installment of the series, we follow the adventures of David, John, and Amy as they expiriment with a new sentient drug called Soy Sauce. This drug is lethal to all who use it, eventually. The trip allows whoever takes it to slip through dimensions and time, leaving them susceptible to creatures such as Wig Monsters, and the cocky Korrok, who plans on taking over our universe.

The side effects don't last that long. The effects will last the rest of my life, I think. Maybe longer..
